Grid

The screen is broken into four main areas.

At the top is the summary of basic state of the game - who's turn it is, or who won the game and messages from the last play.

Along the left are the players of this game, whether they are alive or dead and their scores.

Below that, if it is your turn to play, are the available actions you can take based on moves left and whether you are attacking or defending. Also, this is where you can surrender or start rematches.

Finally, the main part of the screen shows a grid of yourself or your opponent as chosen.

Pick your opponent or yourself on the left. The available actions will appear under the opponents. The chosen player will be highlighted.

Picking an opponent.

To defend, click 'Their view of you.' above the grid. This will show what your opponent can see. Highlight the spot you want to defend. Then pick the action you want to take on the left under the opponents.

The attack, click 'Your view of them.' above the gird. This will show your view of their sea so far. If they are not already defeated, you can click the cell you wish to attack and the choice of attack on the left.

Picking yourself.

This will show you a consolidated view of what your combined opponents can see. Depending on game options, not every player may see the same view. You can click on each player to see their specific view.

Grid Highlights

When you pick a spot on the grid, it will be highlighted like . Additional cells will be highlighted to show what will be shown or hidden if you use Spy or ECM like . Also, if you are selecting a cell with your own ship, it will be highlighted as well to show which ship will take Evasive Maneuvers or make Emergency Repairs.

What do the different images mean?

The Unknown.

This area is not known.
This ship is damaged, but the damage is not known to the current opponent.

The Known.

This area is known to have an undamaged ship.
This area is known to have a ship damaged by you.
This area is known to an already damaged ship that you hit as well.
This area is known to have a ship damaged by another player.
This area is known to be empty from a shot taken by yourself.
This area is known to be empty from a miss by another player.
This area is known to be empty.

The Uncertain (from Evasive Maneuvers).

This area was previously known to have an undamaged ship.
This area was previously known to have a ship damaged by you.
This area was previously known to an already damaged ship that you hit as well.
This area was previously known to have a ship damaged by another player.
This area was previously known to be empty from a shot taken by yourself.
This area was previously known to be empty from a miss by another player.
This area was previously known to be empty.
