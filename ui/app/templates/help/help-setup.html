Setup Help

This is where you place your own ships. Once you are happy, press Save.

The game begins when all players have saved their ships. As long as not all players have saved their setup, you can keep moving your ships even after pressing Save.

But move quickly since there is no guarantee the other players won't finish soon!

You can drag and drop your ships into position. The ship you are moving will turn green while you are moving it. Ships cannot be placed on top of each other or out of bounds.

Double clicking or double tapping a ship will rotate it 90 degrees.

If ships are red, it means they are on top of each other and at least one needs to be moved.
